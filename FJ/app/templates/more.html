Welcome to The Squad Journal, where we want you to share your day with your squad! The creators of The Squad Journal wanted to mix two key ideas: journaling and being in groups. We're big on the idea of how consistently carrying out small actions can lead to long term success, such as journaling. Journaling comes with benefits such as a boost in oneâ€™s mood, clarity of mind and the ability to track progress.

We really believe in the idea of journaling in a group as when used correctly, it's a powerful tool. Every member of a group can see each others journal entries which were posted in that specific group. This allows for sharing your day with those who you desire.

Other uses of Squad Journal include tracking progression of projects, keeping up to date with friends/family, sharing ideas and keeping track of fixtures/results for sports tournements etc. We have a friendly interface and an even friendlier customer support team!
